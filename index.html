About us

At Guillot Electrical & Controls, we pride ourselves on delivering personable, high-quality electrical services for a variety of sectors, including residential, commercial, industrial, renewable energy, and mining.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that each project, regardless of size or complexity, is completed to the highest standards. We believe in honest communication and offer competitive rates to provide our clients with the best value.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to ensuring your electrical needs are met with reliability and efficiency, making Guillot Electrical your trusted partner in power.

Electrical Contractor for...

Residential

At Guillot Electrical, we offer comprehensive residential electrical services to ensure your home is safe, efficient, and up to code. From wiring new constructions to upgrading existing systems, our experts provide reliable solutions for all your home electrical needs.

Commercial

Our commercial electrical services are designed to support businesses of all sizes. We handle everything from office lighting and wiring to electrical maintenance and emergency repairs, ensuring your commercial space operates smoothly and efficiently.

Industrial

Specializing in industrial electrical systems, Guillot Electrical delivers robust solutions for factories, plants, and large-scale operations. Our team is equipped to manage high-voltage installations, machinery wiring, and maintenance, prioritizing safety and performance.

Renewable Energy

Embrace sustainable energy solutions with our renewable energy services. We offer design, installation, and maintenance of solar panels, wind turbines, and other renewable systems, helping you reduce your carbon footprint and energy costs.

Mining

In the demanding mining industry, dependable electrical systems are crucial. Guillot Electrical provides specialized services for mining operations, including power distribution, equipment wiring, and safety system installations, ensuring optimal functionality and compliance.
